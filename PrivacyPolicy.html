Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Chronoball: Time to Shoot! ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Chronoball: Time to Shoot! is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • First Launch Information: The app stores a simple flag on your device to determine if this is your first time opening the app, so we can show you the tutorial. This information never leaves your device.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Game scores or statistics
  • Any data that could identify you personally

How We Use Information

The minimal information stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Display the tutorial on first launch
  • Provide a smooth user experience

All game data (scores, game state, etc.) exists only during active gameplay sessions and is not stored or transmitted anywhere. Just like a real match, once the game ends, the data disappears—no permanent record is kept.

Third-Party Services

Chronoball: Time to Shoot! does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and does not connect to external servers. Your timing precision stays on your device, just like your skills stay with you.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults system. We do not transmit, share, or sell any data to third parties. Since we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to secure or protect beyond what Apple's operating system already provides.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect personal information, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Perfect timing knows no age limit!

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can delete the app at any time, which will remove all locally stored data. Clean slate, just like a new match.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
