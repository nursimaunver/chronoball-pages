What is Chronoball?

Chronoball is a precision timing-based football game where every centisecond counts. You must tap at the perfect moment (0, 25, 50, or 75 centiseconds) to score goals, make saves, and control the match. Master the rhythm, master the game!

How do I improve my timing?

Practice makes perfect! Start with longer match durations (70s or 90s) to get comfortable with the timing. Watch the analog chronometer carefully‚Äîthe key is hitting those precise targets: .00 for goals, .25/.75 for possession, and .50 for shots on target.

The app crashes or freezes

Try these steps:

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Check that your device meets the minimum requirements (iOS/watchOS 26.1+)

Sounds or haptics not working

Check your device settings:

  • Ensure your device isn't in Silent Mode
  • Check that system sounds are enabled
  • Verify haptic feedback is enabled in Settings
  • On iPhone, check the side switch isn't set to silent

App won't install or update

This is usually related to App Store or device storage issues:

  • Check you have enough storage space
  • Ensure you're signed into the App Store with the correct Apple ID
  • Try restarting your device
  • Check your internet connection

üéØ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my game data saved?

No, game data is not saved. Each match is independent, just like a real football match. Scores exist only during gameplay and reset when you start a new match.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Chronoball works completely offline. No internet connection required. Perfect for playing anywhere, anytime.

Is there multiplayer?

Yes! Pass-and-play two-player mode lets you challenge friends on the same device. Take turns and see who has the better timing!

How do I share my scores?

After a match, use the share button to create a beautiful scorecard image you can share on social media. Show off your perfect timing!
